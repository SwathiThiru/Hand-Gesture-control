About Us

The GROCERY MART are the shopping stores in the premises of Vellore Institute of Technology,Vellore. They strive towards fulfiling the requirements of the students who stay miles away from their home in the hostels of VIT University. They cater a huge range of products in consumables, beautycare, stationary and other every day essentials. They have a totsl of six shops, three in the men hostels and two in the ladies hostels. The star facility of this chain of shops that distinguishes them from others is the flexibility provided to the customers to purchase without making cash payment each time. The students do not have to carry wallet each time they have to buy. If they forget to bring the wallet, they do not have to go to their rooms and bring money.
